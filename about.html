Who We Are

RC ARGONET

RC ARGONET is a non-profit association established in 2009, headquartered in Banja Luka, Bosnia and Herzegovina. We design and implement projects that drive practical, measurable improvements for communities, institutions, and value chains — bridging European standards and local realities through partnerships, evidence, and hands-on delivery.

We collaborate with public institutions, civil society, academia, and the private sector to turn strategies, policies, and research into real-world solutions — transparent, auditable, and impact-focused.

Sustainable Agriculture Green Transition EU Integration Public Policy Training Digital Tools Transparency & Traceability Data & Accountability Partnerships & Delivery

Our Mission

To improve quality of life and inclusive socio-economic development in Bosnia and Herzegovina and the wider European neighborhood by delivering evidence-based initiatives that strengthen communities, foster innovation, advance skills and employability, and accelerate the transition to a sustainable, digital, and resilient society.

Our Vision

A Bosnia and Herzegovina fully integrated into European innovation, education, and civic ecosystems — where opportunities are equitable, institutions and markets operate transparently and responsibly, and sustainable development is achieved through collaboration, digital transformation, and shared European values.

Our Work Spans

Five Program Units

Sustainable Development & Green Transition

Sustainable agriculture, circular economy, and EU environmental policy implementation (GlobalG.A.P. & Organic standards).

Public Policy & EU Integration

Policy and regulatory analysis, EU alignment, advocacy, and civil society capacity building.

Education & Capacity Building

Training programs, non-formal education, manuals, workshops, and knowledge transfer.

Digitalization, Innovation & Data

Digital transformation, tools for transparency/traceability, and accountability reporting.

Projects & Partnerships

Proposal development, project coordination, consortium management, and donor compliance (USAID, GIZ, EU).

Governance

Organizational Structure

A clear, accountable governance model based on RACI logic ensures effective delivery and transparent reporting across all levels.

Assembly

Accountable — Ultimate Authority

Adopts strategic documents, oversees operations, and reviews annual reports.

President

Accountable & Responsible

Executive leadership, approves work plans, and represents the organization to partners.

Programme Coordinators

Responsible — Task Execution

Plan and execute activities, manage project portfolios, and deliver results.

High Council

Consulted — Strategic Input

Provides strategic recommendations without operational reporting duties.

Partners & Donors

Informed

Receive progress updates through regular project and financial reporting.

Gender Equality

Gender Equality Plan

Our EU-grade Gender Equality Plan (2026–2028) is publicly available and ensures institutional commitment to inclusion, equity, and a safe working environment.

Committed to Equality & Inclusion

RC ARGONET's GEP is aligned with Horizon Europe expectations — featuring dedicated resources, gender-disaggregated data collection, staff training, and measurable actions. The plan covers all staff, contractors, and anyone representing us in projects and events.

With zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, and retaliation, we embed gender mainstreaming at every level — from governance and recruitment to project design and delivery.
