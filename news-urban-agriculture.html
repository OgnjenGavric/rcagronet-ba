Announcement: Urban Agriculture Open Day in Banja Luka

Sep 25, 2018 By RC ARGONET

The Faculty of Agriculture of the University of Banja Luka, in cooperation with the General Agricultural Cooperative “Sunčani Brijeg,” is organizing an Open Day dedicated to urban agriculture.

The event will be held on September 25th at 11:00 AM within the University Campus in Banja Luka. This activity is part of the Local Partnership for Employment Project, aimed at promoting local products from project beneficiaries and cooperative members.

The Concept of Urban Agriculture

The focus of the event is the “Urban Agriculture” project, which aims to develop and implement urban farming as a model for employment. Urban agriculture involves the cultivation, processing, and distribution of food within or near populated areas, contributing to:

  • Local food production and food security.
  • Creation of new employment opportunities.
  • Sustainable community development.

Partners and Implementation

The project is implemented by the Faculty of Agriculture in collaboration with several key partners:

  • City of Banja Luka
  • Employment Bureau of Republika Srpska
  • Caritas Switzerland (Sarajevo)
  • Mushrooms Trade d.o.o. (Laktaši)
  • RC ARGONET (Banja Luka)

RC ARGONET continues to support initiatives that contribute to the development of sustainable agriculture, local economy, and new employment opportunities through its participation in local partnerships.
