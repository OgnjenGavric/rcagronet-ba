Steering Board Meeting of the Local Employment Partnership in Banja Luka

Jun 13, 2018 By RC ARGONET

A meeting of the Steering Board of the Local Partnership for Employment Banja Luka was held today, focusing on key activities and future steps in the implementation of partner initiatives.

The discussion covered several vital topics related to the development of the local agricultural and employment sectors, ensuring coordination among all involved stakeholders.

Key Topics of the Meeting

Partners engaged in detailed reports and planning regarding:

  • The current progress of agricultural production.
  • The organization and strengthening of agricultural cooperatives.
  • The development of the Urban Farm (Experimental Field).
  • Certification of production according to organic production standards.
  • Market development and access strategies for local producers.

Meeting Participants

The meeting brought together representatives from institutional and organizational partners, including:

  • RS Employment Bureau
  • Grad Banja Luka
  • Faculty of Agriculture, University of Banja Luka
  • RC ARGONET
  • GOPA mbH / YEP
  • Caritas Switzerland

RC ARGONET continues its active participation in the partnership, focusing on supporting organic production development, certification processes, and enhancing the market capacity of local farmers.
